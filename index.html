About

Founded in 2021 by a dynamic trio of female entrepreneurs, Rocki Pocki represents the perfect blend of passion, artistry, and a love for the captivating simplicity of nature.

At Rocki Pocki, we have taken the timeless allure of geological wonders and carefully curated a collection of exquisite pocket-sized rocks, each one embodying a piece of the Earth's rich history. Our rocks are not just possessions, they are companions that you can carry with you, fitting perfectly into the rhythm of your life.

From the mountainous ranges of the Alps to the serene riverbeds of Asia, our carefully selected pieces speak volumes of their origins. Every Rocki Pocki stone encapsulates a unique story waiting to be discovered, turning the act of carrying a rock in your pocket into a personal journey of exploration.
